About Me

Hey there! I'm Andreas Johansson, a solo indie game developer passionate about creating fantasy-inspired worlds and experimenting with game mechanics.

Right now, I'm working on PixelJump, a 2D platformer where I'm exploring unique movement mechanics and level design using Unity.

Game development is my creative outlet, and I enjoy learning new things, refining mechanics, and sharing my progress. Whether it’s ARPG combat, MMO-inspired worlds, or just a small passion project, I’m always diving into new ideas.

This site is where I document my journey, share updates, and hopefully connect with others who love making and playing games.
